help

where do i buy The Sims 4?
you can download The Sims 4 for pc/mac through Origin or Steam. i bought mine through Origin.
where do i download/find custom content and mods for The Sims 4?
i, personally, like to find custom content and script mods through Tumblr. MaxisMatchCCWorld is a great Tumblr blog to start with as they have a pretty extensive masterlist in their navigation tag.
i don't typically download off of the site called simsdom, and thesimsresource can be a bit much with the ads, but that's my personal preference. always be careful when downloading anything even if it isn't mods.
how do i use custom content and mods for The Sims 4?
after downloading, you'll want to move the files to your mods folder. SimplyJustSims has a short tutorial/guide on how to do this. itsmeTroi on YouTube has a more indepth video on installing custom content that i'll include below.

happy simming!
